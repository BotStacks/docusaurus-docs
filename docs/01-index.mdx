1. **Sign up or log in** at [dashboard.botstacks.ai](https://dashboard.botstacks.ai)
2. **Read the [Quickstart Guide](/getting-started/quickstart-guide/create-botstack)**
3. **Explore Sequence Studio** to design your first assistant
