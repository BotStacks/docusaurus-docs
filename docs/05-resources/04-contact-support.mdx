## When to Contact Support

- **Technical Issues:** Something’s not working as expected? Email us or open a support ticket.
- **Billing & Accounts:** Questions about plans, billing, or subscriptions? Reach out via email or the contact form.
- **Feature Requests:** Share ideas for new features or improvements.
- **Community Help:** For quick answers or advice from other users, Discord is your best bet.
